About Maths Paradise

Our Story

Maths Paradise began in 1997 as a small neighbourhood tuition class. Over the years, we've expanded our reach and developed a structured approach that helps students understand mathematics conceptually — not just memorize steps. Our founder (your dad) brings patience and an ability to break tough topics into simple, memorable tricks.

What we teach

From basics to exam-level problem solving:

  • Number sense & mental math
  • Ratio, Proportion and Percentages
  • Algebra: expressions, equations and inequalities
  • Geometry: shapes, areas and volumes
  • Trigonometry basics for upper grades
